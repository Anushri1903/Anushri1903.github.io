About Me

I'm a Vice President in Model Risk / Financial Crime Analytics at Citi. My work centers on model validation, performance evaluation, and risk assessment, ensuring the models behind critical financial decisions are robust, explainable, and hold up to regulatory and business scrutiny.

I work daily with large, complex datasets, using Python, SQL, and statistical techniques to translate model behavior into insights stakeholders can act on with confidence.

What I'm most interested in is the combination of predictive and prescriptive modeling — not just forecasting what's likely to happen, but using that insight to guide the best course of action. That intersection, applied to financial analytics, is where I'd like to keep growing.
